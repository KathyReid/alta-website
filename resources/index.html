Internship, Funding and Collaboration Opportunities

We would like to track possible funding and collaboration opportunities for the Australian Language Technology community. If you would like to offer a collaboration, or become aware of a funding opportunity, please let us know at alta-tech@googlegroups.com.
  • Xerox Research Centre Europe (based in Grenoble, France) has a strong competency in language technology: from parsing and machine translation to data mining and sentiment analysis. XRCE offers internships for registered students and typically lists new opportunities close to the start of the year, though considers spontaneous applications. Open internships and further information available at http://www.xrce.xerox.com/About-XRCE/Internships
  • The Australian e-Health Research Centre at CSIRO offers Language Technology-related internship opportunities for students and summer vacation/honours/Masters/PhD scholarships in the areas of clinical terminology/ontologies and medical text analytics. Please contact Anthony Nguyen (firstname.surname@csiro.au), Research Stream Leader for further information.
  • The Australian Research Council National Competitive Grants Program is the primary source of research funding for research in Australia, across disciplines.
  • The Australian Army Research and Development Scheme has a number of topics that might be suitable for language technology researchers for FY2013/2014.
