Submissions and Reviewing Process

To submit a paper, you first need to sign up for a new user account if you do not already have an account (use the link near the top of the submission system login page). When you have an account, sign in and upload your paper. You may edit or upload new versions of your paper until the submission deadline.

Go to the Online Submission and Reviewing System.

For any comments or questions about the workshop please contact the organizers at workshop@alta.asn.au.
