Accommodation

Information about the accommodation can be found on the Women's College website . We have secured a pretty good deal with them, so we think it will be hard to find a better (and more convenient option). We have a block reservation for around 40 rooms. You can book the Women's College as part of the registration form (including pre-payment).

We have not arranged alternative accommodation in the city, but most of the hotels in the city centre will be convenient either via bus or taxi (or a 30 minute walk).
