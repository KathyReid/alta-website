Information for presenters

For those of you presenting regular papers at ALTW 2005, you will be allocated 20 minutes for the presentation and 10 minutes. For poster presentations, you will be provided with a 1800mm x 1200mm (landscape orientation) poster board which you are welcome to use as you wish; be warned, however, that if you opt for an A0 poster, it won't fit on the board unless it's in landscape orientation.
