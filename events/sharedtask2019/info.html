Useful Information about the ALTA 2019 Shared Task

This page contains some links that you may find useful when attempting the task. More information may be added later on.

Related Papers

  1. Aditya Joshi, Pranav Goel, Pushpak Bhattacharyya, and Mark Carman. "Sarcasm Target Identification: Dataset and An Introductory Approach." Proceedings of the Eleventh International Conference on Language Resources and Evaluation (LREC-2018). 2018.
  2. Mikhail Khodak, Nikunj Saunshi, and Kiran Vodrahalli. "A Large Self-Annotated Corpus for Sarcasm." Proceedings of the Eleventh International Conference on Language Resources and Evaluation (LREC-2018). 2018.

Some Ideas

  • Given the small amount of training data provided, you may want to incorporate additional training data such as those mentioned in the list of related papers.
