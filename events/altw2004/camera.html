ALTW 2004: Instructions for camera-ready papers

Your camera-ready version is due on the 1st of November 2004

Please remove the keywords and fax number from your camera copy

We are using the COLING 2004 style. The corresponding style files can be found at the workshop website as a zip file:

http://www.alta.asn.au/events/altw2004/format/camera-stylefiles/templatesColing04.zip

*** Please note that the style files have been updated and the new style files *must* be used ***

The length is 8 pages. Papers that exceed this limit will not be published and will not be included in the conference program. Camera-ready versions should be single-spaced in 11 point font. The final camera-ready version of the paper must be formatted for A4-sized paper.

The first page of your submission should include:

paper title,
author name(s) and affiliation,
complete addresses including email address,
abstract.

Only PDF files will be accepted. Therefore you are encouraged to send an early version with the typographical complexity of your final intended version so that we can check it is printable. Camera-ready papers should be sent to workshop AT alta.asn.au .
