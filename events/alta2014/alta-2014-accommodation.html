ALTA 2014 Accommodation

The following are suggestions only. Prices are indicative and may be subject to availability. To request the following prices, please mention "RMIT" in your booking. Please make enquiries and booking directly with the hotel.

Accommodation options:
  • Low budget: Tune on Swanston St has RMIT rates: double for $100 and a twin share for $110 per night (students might consider sharing a room at Tune). Mention RMIT in the booking and you'll receive the $11 per night utilities package for free (includes access to a television).
  • Medium budget: Ibis (between RMIT and the Victoria Markets) has an RMIT corporate rate of $130.
  • High budget: Rydges (near University of Melbourne) has a corporate rate of $185 per night.
