This page contains some links that participants may find useful when attempting the task. More information may be added later on.

  • Strength of recommendation taxonomy (SORT): a patient-centered approach to grading evidence in the medical literature. By Ebell, Siwek, Weiss, Woolf, Susman, Ewigman, and Bowman, 2004.
