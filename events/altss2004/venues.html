ALTSS and ALTW 2004 Venues

The venue of the summer school is the Department of Computing of Macquarie University. This is located at building E6A within the campus of Macquarie University.

The venue of the workshop is the MGSM Executive Hotel and Conference Centre. This is located at building E12A within the campus of Macquarie University.

  • MGSM map

Parking

Macquarie University operates a "pay and display" parking system on campus, between 6am and 8pm, seven days a week. Each vehicle must display a valid parking ticket. Parking tickets can be obtained from ticket machines and must be displayed on the driver's side of the vehicle.
