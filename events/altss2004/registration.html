ALTSS 2004 Registration

This year the workshop will be held as part of SST 2004. In consequence there are two registration forms, one for the summer school and another one for the workshop/SST.

  • Summer School registration form
  • Workshop registration - link to SST registration page

Please note the following:

  • Early registration closes on the 31st October. After that date there is an extra charge.
  • There is a discounted rate for ASSTA members, you must be a paid up ASSTA member for 2004 to take advantage of this rate. We will check with our current membership list, please see the ASSTA website for details of how to renew your membership or become a member.
  • To qualify for the student rate you must be a currently enrolled tertiary student, please include a copy of your student ID for confirmation.
  • If you are retired or in reciept of a pension, you may register at the student rate. Please provide some documentation with your registration.
  • Closing date for registration for the summer school is 15 November 2004.
  • Closing date for registration for the workshop is 22 November 2004.
