Sponsorship

The Australasian Language Technology Association (ALTA) welcomes the possibility that organisations or institutions might wish to provide full or part support for the attendance of postgraduate students at either or both the ALTA Summer School or Workshop. The organisers are happy to discuss flexible registration options to include such support; please contact Diego Molla.

A number of sponsorship opportunities exist; for more information contact Dominique Estival.

Bronze Level: AU$500

  • logo on conference website
  • logo on all published materials (CD-ROM, program, etc)
  • publicity during breaks throughout conference
  • insert in delegates' satchels

Silver Level: AU$1000

  • logo on conference website
  • logo on all published materials (CD-ROM, program, etc)
  • publicity during breaks throughout conference
  • insert in delegates' satchels
  • single participant registration for one day/one course

Gold Level: AU$2000

  • logo on conference website
  • logo on all published materials (CD-ROM, program, etc)
  • publicity during breaks throughout conference
  • insert in delegates' satchels
  • display area at venue
  • single participant registration for Summer School and Workshop

Platinum Level: AU$4000

  • logo on conference website
  • logo on all published materials (CD-ROM, program, etc)
  • publicity during breaks throughout conference
  • insert in delegates' satchels
  • display area at venue
  • two participant registrations for Summer School and Workshop
